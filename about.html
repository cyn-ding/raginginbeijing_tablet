About

Hi, I'm Cynthia.

I believe that information should be accessible and equitable for all. As a global citizen, I'm particularly interested in exploring connections between cultures and designing messages that can effectively transcend words. As a designer, I hope to bridge communities and tell meaningful stories to explore various identities. I'm passionate about finding growth through helping others grow. My goal is to be able to follow my dreams, connect the people I reach to greater opportunities, and win with integrity.

I work on illustrations in my spare time and am enthusiastic about finding the best happy hour deals wherever I am. I spend too much time looking at memes and am surprisingly good at claw machines. I enjoy watching nature documentaries and welcome all new experiences.

Contact for collaboration, project inquiries, or good conversation.
